# [here](https://github.com/z3300/RetroTV) is the code for this project.

i made a my own custom 24/7 tv with a raspberry pi and an old crt.

the idea came from casey neistat’s studio. i remember watching his vlogs and seeing a monitor in the background always playing old movies. i thought it was kinda weird and wondered how it worked.

in a mkbhd setup tour he mentions having a monitor that loops the godfather I and II.  

from what i remembered i thought it was something more sophisticated, like an actual tv system running in the background. turns out it was just a screensaver.  

but i still wanted to build something real, more like a tv station running nonstop with stuff i liked.

the first version was coded pretty simply: just using vlc to play videos on a loop randomly. but i wanted to make it more custom and more tv-like. so i added things like a tv static simulator, transitions when switching channels, separate channels, logos, and even a little web app to control it.  

i also designed my own logos and edited custom transitions because the ones on youtube weren’t good enough.  

![logo.gif](/images/transition.gif)

once it was running, i filled it with *legally* downloaded movies and shows, plus my favorite stuff: old disney movies, cartoon network shows, anthony bourdain, diners drive-ins and dives, random documentaries, and even early 2000s commercials to make it feel more authentic.
